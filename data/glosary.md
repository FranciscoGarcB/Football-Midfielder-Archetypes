# Glossary and Column Guide: FBref Data

This document explains the meaning of each column contained in your three datasets (`standard_data`, `shooting_data`, and `misc_data`). These metrics represent the industry standard for quantitative football (soccer) analysis.

---

## 1. Identification & Demographic Columns (Common)

These columns appear across all three datasets and serve to identify the player, their team context, and basic demographic information.

* **`player`** *(Player)*: The full name of the football player.
* **`team`** *(Squad)*: The team the player played minutes for during that specific season.
* **`league`** *(Comp)*: The national domestic competition (league) the club participates in (e.g., Premier League, Bundesliga).
* **`season`** *(Season)*: The year or period of the season corresponding to the records (e.g., `1920` for the 2019-2020 season).
* **`nation`** *(Nation)*: The player's nationality (represented by the three-letter FIFA code, e.g., ENG, FRA, GER).
* **`age`** *(Age)*: The player's age during that season.
* **`born`** *(Born)*: The player's year of birth.
* **`90s`** / **`Playing Time_90s`** *(90s)*: Total minutes played divided by 90. This represents the equivalent number of "full 90-minute matches" accumulated (e.g., 1874 minutes / 90 = 20.8 matches). This is the core denominator used to calculate all "Per 90" metrics.

---

## 2. `standard_data` (Standard Statistics)

Contains general information regarding the player's participation on the pitch, including minute distribution, goal volume, assists, and disciplinary cards.

### Playing Time
* **`Playing Time_MP`** *(Matches Played)*: Total matches played. The number of games in which the player stepped onto the pitch (either as a starter or a substitute).
* **`Playing Time_Starts`** *(Starts)*: Matches started. The number of games where the player was in the starting eleven.
* **`Playing Time_Min`** *(Minutes)*: Total minutes accumulated on the pitch during the competition.

### General Performance
* **`Performance_Gls`** *(Goals)*: Total goals scored.
* **`Performance_Ast`** *(Assists)*: Total assists directly leading to a goal scored by a teammate.
* **`Performance_G+A`** *(Goals + Assists)*: Total direct offensive production (The sum of goals and assists).
* **`Performance_G-PK`** *(Non-Penalty Goals)*: Goals scored from open play or set pieces, **excluding** penalties. This measures pure goal-scoring ability without the inflation of being the team's designated penalty taker.
* **`Performance_PK`** *(Penalty Goals)*: Goals scored from the penalty spot.
* **`Performance_PKatt`** *(Penalty Attempts)*: Total penalty kicks taken by the player, regardless of whether they scored or missed.
* **`Performance_CrdY`** *(Yellow Cards)*: Number of yellow cards received.
* **`Performance_CrdR`** *(Red Cards)*: Total red cards received (either direct or via a second yellow card).

### Normalized Statistics (Per 90 Minutes)
These metrics divide total output by the accumulated `90s` to allow fair comparisons between players with different amounts of playing time.
* **`Per 90 Minutes_Gls`**: Average goals scored per 90 minutes of play.
* **`Per 90 Minutes_Ast`**: Average assists provided per 90 minutes of play.
* **`Per 90 Minutes_G+A`**: Average combined goals and assists per 90 minutes.
* **`Per 90 Minutes_G-PK`**: Average non-penalty goals scored per 90 minutes.
* **`Per 90 Minutes_G+A-PK`**: Average combined goals and assists, excluding penalties, per 90 minutes. This is one of the purest metrics for evaluating the regular attacking impact of a midfielder.

## 3. `shooting_data` (Shooting Statistics)

Measures finishing efficiency, shooting volume, and accuracy when targeting the opponent's goal. This is highly useful for evaluating the goal threat generated by an attacking or box-to-box midfielder.

* **`Standard_Gls`**: Total goals scored (retained here as a cross-reference).
* **`Standard_Sh`** *(Shots)*: Total number of shots taken (excluding penalty shootouts). This includes blocked, off-target, and on-target attempts.
* **`Standard_SoT`** *(Shots on Target)*: Shots on target. Remates that were heading into the net and required a goalkeeper save or resulted in a goal.
* **`Standard_SoT%`** *(Shot on Target Percentage)*: Percentage of total shots that hit the target `(Standard_SoT / Standard_Sh)`. Measures a player's shooting precision.
* **`Standard_Sh/90`** *(Shots per 90)*: Average shots attempted per 90 minutes. Measures a player's aggressiveness and volume in seeking out shots.
* **`Standard_SoT/90`**: Average shots on target per 90 minutes.
* **`Standard_G/Sh`** *(Goals per Shot)*: Goals scored per shot taken. This is a metric of clinical finishing efficiency: what percentage of their total shots turn into goals (high values point to a lethal finisher or smart shot selection).
* **`Standard_G/SoT`** *(Goals per Shot on Target)*: Goals scored per shot on target. Indicates how many of the shots that actually hit the target managed to beat the goalkeeper.
* **`Standard_PK`**: Penalty goals scored.
* **`Standard_PKatt`**: Penalties attempted.

---

## 4. `misc_data` (Miscellaneous & Defensive Statistics)

Tracks disciplinary metrics, physical battles, fouls, and defensive actions in intermediate zones of the pitch. For a midfielder (`MF`), this module is critical because it highlights their ball-winning capabilities, positional awareness, and defensive work rate.

* **`Performance_2CrdY`** *(Second Yellow Card)*: The number of times a player was sent off specifically due to receiving a second yellow card in a single match.
* **`Performance_Fls`** *(Fouls Committed)*: Fouls committed. Measures a player's defensive aggression or tactical fouling to disrupt opposition play.
* **`Performance_Fld`** *(Fouls Suffered)*: Fouls suffered/drawn. Indicates a player's ability to protect the ball, carry it under pressure, and draw errors out of opponents to win set-pieces.
* **`Performance_Off`** *(Offsides)*: The number of times the player was caught offside.
* **`Performance_Crs`** *(Crosses)*: Crosses attempted into the box, whether from open play or corner kicks.
* **`Performance_Int`** *(Interceptions)*: Successful interceptions. The number of times a player cut off an opposition pass or play through smart positioning (without needing to make a direct tackle).
* **`Performance_TklW`** *(Tackles Won)*: Tackles won. The number of ground challenges where the player's team successfully regained possession as a direct result of the tackle.
* **`Performance_PKwon`** *(Penalty Won)*: Penalties won. The number of times the player was fouled inside the opposition penalty area, resulting in a penalty kick for their team.
* **`Performance_PKcon`** *(Penalty Conceded)*: Penalties conceded. The number of times the player committed a foul inside their own penalty box, resulting in a penalty kick for the opponent.
* **`Performance_OG`** *(Own Goals)*: Own goals scored by the player.
